# Risk Summary – Botium Toys

**Critical Assets:** Employee devices, servers, POS systems, internal network, ecommerce systems

## Top Risks
1. Non-compliance with PCI DSS → fines & reputational damage  
2. GDPR violations → legal risk  
3. Weak access control → unauthorized access  
4. Lack of disaster recovery & backups → operational downtime  
5. No IDS → undetected security incidents

## Recommendations
1. Implement encryption for cardholder and sensitive data  
2. Enforce least privilege and separation of duties  
3. Establish disaster recovery and backup procedures  
4. Install and configure IDS  
5. Strengthen and enforce password policies centrally  
6. Complete asset inventory and classify critical data  
7. Schedule regular monitoring of legacy systems  
8. Conduct staff training on security policies and compliance
