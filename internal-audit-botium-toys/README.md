# Internal Audit – Botium Toys

## Scenario
Botium Toys is a U.S.-based toy company with a physical storefront and an expanding online presence.  
The IT department requires an internal audit to evaluate compliance, assess risks, and strengthen cybersecurity controls.

## Project Objective
Perform an internal IT audit to:

- Assess existing assets  
- Evaluate controls and compliance practices  
- Identify risks and vulnerabilities  
- Provide recommendations for improving the company’s security posture

## Tools & Frameworks
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (NIST CSF)  
- Internal audit controls and compliance checklist methodology  
- Risk assessment scoring  

## Tasks Completed
1. Reviewed IT manager’s scope, goals, and risk assessment  
2. Completed step-by-step controls and compliance checklist  
3. Summarized risks, vulnerabilities, and compliance gaps  
4. Developed professional recommendations

## Skills Demonstrated
- Internal audit execution  
- Risk assessment and scoring  
- Compliance evaluation (PCI DSS, GDPR, SOC frameworks)  
- Security controls review and documentation
